Privacy Policy for PackSnap

Effective Date: July 11, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to PackSnap. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy governs the privacy policies and practices of our application.

2. Information We Collect

PackSnap processes video files recorded through your device's camera. These files are stored locally on your device.

If you choose to enable the Google Drive Sync feature, PackSnap will request access to your Google Drive via the https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.file scope.

3. How We Use Google Drive Data

We use the Google Drive API exclusively to:

  • Upload packing videos you record directly into a specific folder named "PackSnap Backups" in your personal Google Drive.
  • Read the storage limit of your Google Drive to warn you if it is full.
  • We do not access, read, or modify any other files in your Google Drive that were not created by PackSnap.
  • We do not share your Google Drive data with any third parties or our own servers. The data goes directly from your device to your Google Drive.

4. Data Security

Your videos are transmitted securely to Google Drive using HTTPS encryption. We do not store your videos on our own servers.
