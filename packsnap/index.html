About PackSnap

PackSnap is a dedicated mobile application designed specifically for e-commerce sellers and online business owners. We help sellers record, organize, and manage packing proof videos with ease. By scanning shipping labels, PackSnap automatically names and categorizes the videos, ensuring sellers always have concrete video evidence to protect their business against fraudulent buyer claims.

Why We Use Google Drive

High-quality packing videos consume a massive amount of local phone storage very quickly. To solve this, PackSnap integrates seamlessly with Google Drive. We request access to your Google Drive via the drive.file scope to automatically backup these heavy video files into a dedicated "PackSnap Backups" folder. This allows sellers to safely free up their phone's local storage while keeping their vital business evidence secure and accessible from anywhere.
