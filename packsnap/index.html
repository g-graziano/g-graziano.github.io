Frequently Asked Questions

What is PackSnap?

PackSnap is a dedicated mobile application designed for e-commerce sellers to easily record and organize packing proof videos by scanning shipping labels. It helps protect businesses from fraudulent buyer claims.

How does the Google Drive backup work?

PackSnap connects securely to your personal Google Drive and automatically uploads your packing videos to a dedicated "PackSnap Backups" folder. This keeps your phone storage free while securing your vital video proof in the cloud.

What is the difference between PackSnap Free and Pro?

The Free version allows you to scan, record, and manually backup packing videos to Google Drive. The Pro version unlocks advanced automation: high-speed Auto-Next packing workflow, Auto-Dim screen for battery saving, silent video recording (mute microphone), forced beep confirmation volume in noisy warehouses, auto-deletion of old local files (7/14/30 days), and packer team member management.

Does PackSnap store my videos on private servers?

No. PackSnap does not store any of your videos on its own servers. Your video proof is strictly confidential and goes directly from your mobile device to your private Google Drive.
